Primary and secondary summary

What is plotted?

The impact parameter b/R is the normalized height where a parallel ray enters the droplet. The incident angle α follows from b/R = sin(α). Order 1 is the primary rainbow, order 2 is the secondary rainbow, and higher orders add more internal reflections.

Uniformly spaced rays in the sketch correspond to uniformly spaced b/R values on the curve. Extrema mark where nearby rays bunch together, producing a bright rainbow direction.
